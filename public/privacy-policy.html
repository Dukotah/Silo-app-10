Privacy Policy

Short version: SILO does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Everything you write, track, or earn stays on your device. The only exception is payment processing, handled by RevenueCat and Apple / Google — covered below.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. SILO has no user accounts, no registration, no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, and no server of any kind.

All data generated by the app — journal entries, task logs, XP progress, streak counts, skill tree unlocks, signal check-ins, and idle-game state — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (via browser localStorage or Capacitor Preferences). It never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up yourself (e.g., through iCloud device backup).

3. How Your Data Is Stored

SILO persists data in the following local storage keys on your device:

  • silo_core_v4 — XP, tasks, journal entries, achievements
  • silo_clarity_v1 — Idle clicker state, generators, prestige
  • silo_echo_v1 — Echo currency and prestige perks
  • silo_skilltree_v1 — Unlocked skill tree nodes
  • silo_signal_v1 — Daily signal check-in history
  • silo_vip_v1 — VIP entitlement status (mirrored locally from RevenueCat)

This data exists only on your device. Deleting the app removes all of it.

4. In-App Purchases & RevenueCat

SILO offers an optional VIP upgrade, which is processed through Apple In-App Purchases (iOS) or Google Play Billing (Android), powered by RevenueCat as our subscription management layer.

When you make a purchase, Apple or Google processes your payment using their own systems. RevenueCat receives a purchase receipt and an anonymous identifier to verify your entitlement. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment card details.

RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy
Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into SILO is RevenueCat (for purchase verification). No advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, social login providers, or tracking libraries are used.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts at runtime when an internet connection is available. Google Fonts does not associate font requests with your identity. See Google's privacy policy for details.

6. Children's Privacy

SILO does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is suitable for users of all ages from a data-privacy perspective. Parents should review the in-app purchase section above.

7. Your Rights

Because all data is local to your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete your data by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app. No request to us is required.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
